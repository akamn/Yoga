About Amardeep Kumar

Amardeep Kumar is a dedicated trainer with expertise in martial arts, dance, and yoga. He holds a black belt in martial arts and is known for his dynamic teaching style. Alongside his passion for martial arts, Amardeep is a skilled dancer and a certified yoga instructor.

He earned his degree in Physical Training from Pune University, where he developed a deep understanding of fitness and wellness.

At our academy, Amardeep Kumar leads a team of passionate trainers who are committed to helping individuals achieve their fitness goals through holistic training programs.
